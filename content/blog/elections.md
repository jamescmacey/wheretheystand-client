## Update

You're on this page because you tried to visit a WhereTheyStand election results site.

WhereTheyStand has been significantly refreshed recently, and as part of this update, election results have been temporarily removed.  Soon, they will be integrated into the main WhereTheyStand website which will be ready to show results for the upcoming 2026 general election.

Until then, the following sites have been shelved:

* elections.wheretheystand.nz
* tauranga.wheretheystand.nz
* hamilton-west.wheretheystand.nz
* port-waikato.election.wheretheystand.nz
